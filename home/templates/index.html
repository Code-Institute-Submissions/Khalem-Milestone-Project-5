What We Do

Here at Praesidio, we want to provide support to endangered species around the world. We are a charitable organisation and do not make any profit off this.

None of this would be possible without the support of people like you! With your help, we can do extraordinary things to help these endangered animals!

What You Can Do to Help

Pay to Adopt

You can adopt an animal with a one time payment. You can view our adoption packs on any animal page!

Preserve Habitats

With your generous payment, we will be able to preserve habitats and stop poachers from endangering these animals.

Recieve Gifts

In exchange for your generosity, you will recieve some gifts! These may range from a postcard to a teddy bear! You can view what gifts you recieve on any adoption pack.
